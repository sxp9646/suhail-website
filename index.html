About me

I am a passionate programmer and engineer with great curiosity for knowledge in my field. I have a large repertoire of computer and electronics skills. I moved from Bangkok City, Thailand in 2013 to pursue an Associate in Engineering at Monroe Community College then a Bachelor of Computer Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology. At RIT, I strive to learn as much as I can and, through interactions with peers and professors, and by taking a full course load, strive to make the most out of my RIT experience.


Sometimes being extremely involved in engineering and academics can be extremely taxing. Therefore, I ensure to keep myself involved in extra-curricular activities both on and off campus. Some of my sporting interests include soccer, cricket and tennis. I have been an avid Manchester United F.C. fan for 15 years and counting so if you ever want to chat about code, soccer or anything else, do not hesitate to reach out!
